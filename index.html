Kannur Accredited by NAAC (Cycle-3) With 'A'

Sir Syed College Taliparamba The college, named after Sir Syed Ahmed Khan, the founder of Aligarh Muslim University, was started in 1967 as a junior college under the University of Kerala. It came under the University of Calicut in 1968 and under the Kannur University in 1996. The institution was reaccredited by NAAC with A grade in 2017.
